About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Dipartimento di Matematica e Informatica "Ulisse Dini" of the Università degli Studi di Firenze under the supervision of Gianmarco Bet and Luisa Andreis. I defended my Phd at université de Bretagne Occidentale in 2022 under the supervision of Françoise Pène.
